MALIGNANI

GOAL

The aim of this project was to create a design concept for the official merchandise of ISIS Arturo Malignani in Udine. My goal was to develop items that reflect school pride, creativity, and inclusivity while incorporating elements that resonate with the student's identity and the institution's legacy.
